# What's New in Documentation Hub

Welcome! This page highlights the most recent improvements to Documentation Hub
and walks you through two new features: **Nuxeo Feedback** and **Document
Managers**. No technical knowledge needed — just plain steps you can follow.

---

## Part 1: Recent Improvements

### Easier-to-read display

Text and labels are clearer and larger in places, and colors now have stronger
contrast so important information stands out. These changes make the app easier
to use for anyone who has a harder time reading fine detail. On-screen
notification messages also stay visible a little longer and are easier to read,
so you have more time to notice them before they disappear.

### Clearer buttons and controls

Buttons are easier to see and click. When you move through the app using your
keyboard, it's now easier to tell which item is currently selected or in focus,
so you always know where you are on the screen.

### Better document comparison

Comparing a document before and after processing is more reliable. The
comparison window now opens and positions itself correctly, including when you
use a second monitor or a high-resolution display. You can also choose how
comparisons are shown from the Settings area.

### More consistent document processing

Documents are processed more consistently across the board. Spacing, lists, and
formatting are handled more reliably — for example, spacing between items, list
levels, table formatting, and blank lines all behave more predictably, so your
finished documents look the way you expect.

## Part 2: Nuxeo Feedback & Document Managers

These two new features let you save your own collection of web links and reach
them quickly from anywhere in the app. They appear both in the **Settings** area
(where you manage them) and in the **sidebar** (where you click them to open).

### What they are for

- **Nuxeo Feedback** — A place to store links to the feedback forms or pages
  you use to report issues or share suggestions. Save them once and you can
  always get to them without searching.
- **Document Managers** — A place to store links to the document-management
  tools, sites, or systems you work with. Keep your most-used destinations one
  click away.

Each one is simply a personal, named list of web links. Pick whichever fits the
kind of link you're saving.

### How to find them

1. Open the **Settings** screen (from the sidebar, or use the Settings option
   at the bottom of the navigation menu).
2. In the list of settings groups, look for the **Quick Links** group.
3. Under it you'll see two sections: **Nuxeo Feedback** and **Document
   Managers**. Click either one to open its editor.

### How to add and configure a link

1. Open either the **Nuxeo Feedback** or **Document Managers** section as
   described above.
2. Click the **Add Link** button. A new, empty link row appears.
3. In the **URL Name** field, type a short, friendly name for the link — this is
   the label you'll see in the sidebar (for example, "Feedback Form" or
   "Team Document Library").
4. In the **Hyperlink** field, type or paste the web address for the link. If
   you leave off the beginning of the address, the app will fill it in for you
   automatically when you save.
5. Repeat for as many links as you'd like to add.
6. Click **Save Settings** to keep your changes. A confirmation appears on the
   button when the save succeeds.

If a web address can't be read, a short message appears beneath that field.
Correct the highlighted address, then save again.

### How to reorder your links

The order you set is the order the links appear in the sidebar, so you can put
your most-used links at the top.

1. Each link row has an **up arrow** and a **down arrow** on its right side.
2. Click the **up arrow** to move a link higher in the list, or the **down
   arrow** to move it lower.
3. Click **Save Settings** when you're happy with the order.

### How to remove a link

1. Each link row has a **trash icon** on its right side.
2. Click it to delete that link. Deletions are saved right away — there's no
   need to click Save Settings afterward.

### What you get as a result

Once you've saved at least one link, your links appear in the **sidebar** under
the **Nuxeo Feedback** and **Document Managers** headings, listed in the order
you arranged them.

- Click a saved link in the sidebar to open it in your default web browser.
- Click the **Nuxeo Feedback** or **Document Managers** heading itself to jump
  straight back to that section in Settings, where you can add, edit, reorder,
  or remove links at any time.

That's it — your most important links are now always within easy reach.
